The system is pretty simple - if your answer is correct, your flashcard is moved to the next box. If the answer is incorrect your flashcard returns to the first box, no matter if you fail at level 2, 3, 4 or 5.

Do you succeed at level 5? It means that you have learnt the material from that flashcard - it will not be in use anymore. Congratulations!
