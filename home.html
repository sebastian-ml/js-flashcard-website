The system is pretty simple - if your answer is correct, your flashcard is moved to the next box. If the answer is incorrect your flashcard returns to the first box, no matter if you fail at level 2, 3, 4 or 5. This system was invented by the German science journalist Sebastian Leitner. Check the Wikipedia article to learn more.

Do you succeed at level 5? It means that you have learnt the material from that flashcard - it will not be in use anymore. Congratulations!

Discover various types of flashcards

  • Classic - Question and correct answer on the card reverse
  • Millionaire - 4 possible answers. Only 1 is correct
  • Write - Fill the missing place with the right answer
  • Gap - Similar to Write but you do not need to write anything, just check the right answer
